Automated Crowd Management for a Safer Tomorrow

CrowdGuard provides real-time monitoring, automated access control, and instant alerts to prevent overcrowding and enhance public safety.

Watch Prototype Demo

The Dangers of Overcrowding

Safety Risks

Crowded spaces increase the risk of stampedes, accidents, and rapid spread of illnesses.

Inefficient Manual Counting

Manual counting is prone to human error, slow, and requires significant staffing resources.

Compliance Violations

Exceeding legal capacity limits can lead to heavy fines and operational shutdowns.

Introducing CrowdGuard: Smart, Simple, Secure

CrowdGuard is an IoT-based system that automates the entire crowd management process. From counting individuals to controlling access, it ensures your space remains safe and compliant without manual intervention.

A Four-Step Process

1

Sense & Count

An overhead camera with computer vision accurately counts every person entering and leaving.

2

Process in Real-Time

A central microcontroller continuously compares the current count against the pre-set capacity limit.

3

Automate Access

If the limit is reached, the system automatically engages an electronic lock, securing the entrance.

4

Alert Instantly

Simultaneously, an alert (SMS/Push Notification) is sent to all registered staff members.

Core Features

Real-Time Occupancy Tracking

A live counter visible on a local dashboard.

Automated Door Control

Hands-free locking and unlocking based on capacity.

Multi-Channel Alerts

Instant notifications via SMS and a local alarm.

High Accuracy Counting

Utilizes advanced sensors to minimize errors.

Scalable & Easy to Install

A modular design that fits any entryway.
